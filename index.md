PhD student in Computer Science at Loyola University Chicago working on applied machine learning for medical imaging, with an emphasis on human-aligned image quality assessment and clinical validation.

## Research focus
- Medical imaging ML for image enhancement, denoising, and synthesis (CT/CBCT workflows)
- Human-aligned evaluation for image quality and clinical usability
- Interpretability and reliability assessment for clinical-facing ML
